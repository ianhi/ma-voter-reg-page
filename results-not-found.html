Who can register to vote in Massachusetts?

‚úì You must be a U.S. citizen
‚úì You must be a resident of Massachusetts
‚úì You must be at least 16 years old (can vote at 18)
‚úì You must not be currently incarcerated for a felony conviction

If you meet these requirements, you are eligible to register to vote!

Important voter registration deadlines:

Registration deadline: You must register at least 20 days before an election to vote in that election.

Early voting: Massachusetts offers early voting for most elections. Check with your local election office for dates and locations.

Election Day registration: Same-day registration is not available in Massachusetts. Make sure to register before the deadline!
